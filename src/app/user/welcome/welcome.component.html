How to apply

1. Complete the online application and upload the required documents to validate your application.

  • Child’s birth certificate.
  • Copy of parents ID’s or passports (for non-Egyptians).
  • In case your child is "transfer student" upload an official report for the past 2 school years.

2. Pay the admission fees with one of the available payment services.

  • Pay with Fawry and upload receipt.
  • Transfer admission fees to one of our bank accounts and upload receipt.
  • Pay directly with credit card.

3. Our admission office will contact parents shortly after receiving the online application in order to set a date and time for interview assessments. Parents should deliver these required documents in the day of assessments:

  • 6 passport size photos
  • 2 original birth certificate
  • Copy of both parents ID’s (Egyptians)
  • Copy of the student’s and both parents passport (Non-Egyptians)
  • Copy of vaccination record
  • Official school report for the past 2 school years (in case of transfer student)

4. The students sit assessment tests which will be specified for each range of age.

5. An email of acceptance will be sent for the students who have passed the assessment test, also the students who have failed the assessment test will be advised to do a retest.

6. For the students who have been accepted, parents will be notified with a registration fees that have to be paid within a week to finish the registration process.

Admission Policy

  • In case parents fill an application without paying the admission fees they will be notified within 2 day to pay the fees, if the parents didn’t pay the fees, the application will be ignored until they pay the admission fees.
  • The admission fee is non-refundable.
